About

Giovanni Pagani founded his studio in 1996 in Parma with the aim of offering architectural and design solutions driven by unconventional elegance.

He collaborates with international brands including B&B Italia, Armani Casa and many others.

Strong multicultural influences enrich his creative universe including cinema, painting and music.
